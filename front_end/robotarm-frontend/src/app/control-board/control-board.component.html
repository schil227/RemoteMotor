double_arrow Welcome to robotarm.io
Control a robot arm remotely with an intuitive user interface. Adjust the sliders and hit 'Engage' to issue your commands. The robotic arm will move periodiaclly based on the average of the input it receives from all of the commanders.
double_arrow Objective
Get the ball in the hoop!

It seems simple, but lining everything up perfectly is quite tricky. The scoreboard reflects the number of times it's been done - will you revel in glory of seeing that sweet, sweet number increase? Or, are you doomed to spend the rest of your life only wondering?

Note that there will be different objectives in the future - come back sometime and check it out!
